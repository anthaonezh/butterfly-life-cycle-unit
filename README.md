# The Amazing Life Cycle of a Butterfly

A comprehensive thematic unit on the life cycle of a butterfly for K-2 special needs students.

## Target Audience
Kindergarten through Second Grade Students with Special Needs.

## Learning Objectives
- Students will be able to name the four stages of the butterfly life cycle.
- Students will use new vocabulary to describe the stages.
- Students will engage in hands-on activities to model the life cycle.

## Project Structure
- `lesson_plan.md`: Detailed lesson plan with activities.
- `vocabulary.md`: Vocabulary list with definitions and visuals.
- `bibliography.md`: Bibliography of children's literature and science resources.
- `sensory_activities.md`: Additional sensory-based activities.
